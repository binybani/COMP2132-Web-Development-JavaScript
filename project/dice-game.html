How to Play ▾

  • To start, click the 'Roll Dice' button.
  • To advance to the next round, clikc the 'Roll Dice' button again.
  • After three rounds, the game is over and the final scoure is calculated.
  • If you want to play agian, click the 'New Gaem' button or refresh the page.

Rules ▾

  • Each round, you and the computer both roll a pair of dice.
  • Your score for that round will be based on the sum of the dice.
  • If you roll doubles (e.g. a 3 and a 3) then your score is mutiplied by 2.
  • Rolling a 1 will give you a score of 0 for that round.
  • The player who has the most points at the end of three rounds is the winnter.
